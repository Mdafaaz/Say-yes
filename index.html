To Ayesha, The Queen of My Heart

Ayesha, tum meri zindagi ka sabse haseen hisa ho. Tumhara naam lete hi har gham door ho jaata hai.

Tum meri har subah ka ujala ho, har raat ka sukoon ho. Tumhari muskurahat meri duniya hai aur tumhara saath mera asmaan.

Yeh chhoti si jagah sirf tumhare liye hai, jahan har lafz tumse mohabbat karta hai. Har pixel mein tum ho, har rang mein tumhari roshni.

Ayesha, tum ho toh sab kuch hai. Tum bin... kuch bhi nahi.

Hamesha aur sirf tumhara, Afaaz
(Ek dil se likha paighaam, jo hamesha tumhara rahega)
